United Staff Helper

Commands List

United Staff Helper is our custom coded Discord Bot, here's a list of the commands it has.

  • about - Posts an embed with a little bit of info about the bot. ?about
  • ban - Allows you to ban a specified user, with a reason. ?ban [user] [reason]
  • help - DMs you this help menu. ?help
  • kick - Allows you to kick a specified user, with a reason. ?kick [user] [reason]
  • ping - Pings the connection of the bot, with a milliseconds value. ?ping
  • warn - Allows you to warn a user, with a reason. ?warn [user] [reason]
  • eval - Allows you to evaluate Javascript code. ?eval [code] MAINTAINERS ONLY
  • cookie - COOOKIES !!! ?cookie
  • slap - Allows you to slap a specified user. ?slap [user]
  • meme - Posts an embed with a random meme. ?meme
  • roast - Allows you to roast someone. ?roast [user]
  • serverinfo - Posts an embed with some basic Server Info. ?serverinfo
  • 8ball - Answers your question with a random Yes or No answer. ?8ball [question]
  • suggestion - DMs you the link for the suggestion box for the United Staff Helper. ?suggestion
